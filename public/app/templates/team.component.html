Gathering in the heart of Atlanta, the Resurgens is a large group of motivated students who have come together carry out the Resurgens solar home. We have formed six subset teams of skilled individuals from diverse majors to strategically implement our net-zero energy and water plans. The subset teams include electrical engineering, architecture, water industrial design, computing, marketing, and fundraising.

Electrical

Maximizing energy production and minimizing energy waste by using renewable energy. Decentralizing the home energy by implementing solar pv systems to energize appliances throughout the home.

Computing

Using the IoT platform to track energy levels in the house in order to adequately provide energy analysis feedback to residents.

Water

Focusing on designing an in-home water system that will efficiently filter, transport, and store the water used throughout the home.

Fundraising

Exploring financial accommodation options by acquiring sponsors and managing finances throughly for optimal use.

Design

Facilitating branding by improving the user experience with graphic design, visuals, interior design, and user interface.

Architecture

Designing a solar home that will efficiently incorporate the facilities needed to reduce residential environmental impact.
